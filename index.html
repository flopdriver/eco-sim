About This Simulation

This is a true pixel-based ecosystem simulation where every element, from soil particles to plant cells to insects, is simulated at the pixel level. Unlike sprite-based simulations, everything here emerges naturally from simple rules and interactions between individual pixels.

Plants

Plants grow from seeds, developing roots to absorb water and nutrients, stems that reach for light, and leaves that perform photosynthesis.

Water

Water flows realistically with gravity, can be absorbed by soil, and is transported through plants for growth.

Soil

Soil holds moisture and nutrients. It can be dry, wet, or fertile, affecting plant growth and ecosystem health.

Insects

Insects move around, seeking plants to eat. They reproduce when they have enough energy and die when they don't.

Worms

Worms move through soil, eating dead matter and making soil more fertile through aeration.

Weather

The simulation includes a day/night cycle, rain events, and temperature fluctuations that affect all organisms.
